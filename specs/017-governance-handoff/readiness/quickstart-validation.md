## Notes on faithful-implementation decisions

- **Layering**: the cross-repo contract describes `fromWorkModel` folding over
  `ShipSummary`/`VerificationSummary`, but those live in `FS.GG.SDD.Commands`,
  which depends on `FS.GG.SDD.Artifacts`. To keep the projection pure and avoid a
  circular reference, the Commands layer pre-extracts the Artifacts-native
  `ReadinessFacts` / `GovernanceConfigPresence` and passes them in. The boundary
  and declared-facts-only guarantee are unchanged.
- **Determinism (SC-003)** is over an *identical source tree*. A fresh `fsgg-sdd
  init` mints a random project id, so two independently-initialized trees differ
  by design; the test re-produces the handoff over the same tree.
- **US4 (`ship-blocked` readiness)** is asserted at the projection level: the pure
  `fromWorkModel` is total and never refuses, so it produces a handoff carrying a
  blocked disposition + blocking ids with no verdict token. Emission itself follows
  ship's existing "blocked commands write nothing" gate.
